1. Maintenance Calories

   - For Normal
     - Body Weight (lbs) X (14-16) Kcal
   - For Overweight
     - Lean Body Mass (LBM) X (14-16) Kcal

2. Lean Body Mass (LBM)

   - Body Weight (lbs) - Body Fat (lbs)
